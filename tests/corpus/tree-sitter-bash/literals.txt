=== =============================================================================
Literal words


=== =============================================================================
echo a
echo a b
---
(list (command (word "echo") (word "a")) (semi) (command (word "echo") (word "a") (word "b")))

=== =============================================================================
Words with special characters


=== =============================================================================

echo }}}
echo ]]] ===
---

=== =============================================================================
Simple variable expansions


=== =============================================================================

---

=== =============================================================================
